About Us

Why FeedForward?

Every day, tonnes of perfectly good food goes to waste while thousands go hungry. We built FeedForward to bridge this gap — a hyperlocal platform where food sellers connect directly with buyers, powered by community delivery partners right in your neighbourhood.

Started in Hyderabad, now growing across Telangana.
